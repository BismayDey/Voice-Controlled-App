Voice Controlled Web App

Available Commands:

  • Say "hello": Responds with a greeting
  • Say "open Google": Opens Google in a new tab
  • Say "change colour": Changes the background colour
  • Say "play a song": Plays a sample audio
  • Say "scroll down": Scrolls the page down
  • Say "scroll up": Scrolls the page up
  • Say "tell me a joke": Tells a random joke
  • Say "what's the time?": Tells the current time
  • Say "open [website]": Opens the specified website
  • Say "search for [query]": Searches Google for the query
  • Say "show me a fact": Responds with a random fact
  • Say "toggle dark mode": Switches between light and dark themes
